About Me

Deyaa Al-Khatib is an engineer driven by endless curiosity.

I like to understand how things work — and, just as much, how to explain them clearly. Every day, I chase a bit more mastery, trying to uncover the secret recipe of learning and of life itself.

My background is in engineering, but my interests reach across many fields from technology and programming to mathematics and the deeper ideas that shape how we think and build. Right now, I’m exploring how to bring clarity and creativity together in everything I do.

This blog is where I share those explorations — thoughts on the systems, tools, and ideas that move our world forward. I aim to make complex topics feel approachable, without losing their depth or beauty.
